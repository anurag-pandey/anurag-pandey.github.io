{%- endif -%} I have always been excited by seeing the tools that people use in their life. Hence, I am sharing the most important ones that I use.

Apps

  1. Readwise - Outstanding app for reading, collecting documents and tracking the highlights. Spend most of my free time using this.
  2. Arc - Browser for my laptop. Incredibly easy to switching profiles across work and personal. Love the ability to use websites in full screen.
  3. Notion and Notion Calendar - Manage information, calendars and to-dos. Amazing for connecting information and collecting variety of information.
  4. Bitwarden - Great open source password manager that gets the job done. Free plan is much better than free plans of all other competitors.
  5. Spotify - Use it for everything audio. Insane recommendation algorithm.
